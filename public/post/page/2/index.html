What’s the IGO dataset?

The International Organization v2.3 dataset is a rich resource for scholars of international relations. Tracking the existence of international organizations and country memberships in them, the data’s various version have been cited over 450 times. Version 2.3 covers the time period 1815 to 2005, and records information about 529 IGOs and membership information for over two million country-igo-year observations. Before 1965, coding was performed only every five years; thereafter information about IGOs and membership is available on an annual basis.
